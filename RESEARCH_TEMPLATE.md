## Scoring Reminders

**8-10:** Strong public commitment with detailed policies  
**6-7:** Adequate policies, some gaps  
**4-5:** Minimal commitment, significant gaps  
**1-3:** Concerning policies or refusal to disclose  
**0:** No public policy available  

**Confidence Examples:**
- ±0.5 = "Very clear in policy, just checked it"
- ±1.0 = "Good evidence but some ambiguity"
- ±1.5 = "Limited public info, making reasonable inference"
- ±2.0 = "Very little public info, lots of uncertainty"
